Sales and Refunds Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Pricing

All pricing for UniverseM services is displayed in USD and is subject to change without notice. Current pricing can be found on our Pricing page.

2. Payment Methods

We accept major credit cards and payment is processed securely through our payment partners.

3. Subscription Terms

Subscriptions are billed on a monthly basis and will automatically renew unless cancelled. You will be charged on the same date each month based on when you first subscribed.

4. Refund Policy

Due to the digital nature of our service, we do not offer refunds for monthly subscriptions. If you cancel your subscription, you will continue to have access to the service until the end of your current billing period.

5. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

6. Overdue Payments

If payment fails or is overdue, we may suspend or terminate your access to the service until payment is received.

7. Free Trials

Free trials are available for eligible users. No credit card required for trial access. Full terms and conditions apply.

8. Contact Us

For questions about sales, billing, or refunds, please contact us through our Contact page.
